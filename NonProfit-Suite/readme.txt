== Description ==

NonprofitSuite is a comprehensive nonprofit management system designed specifically for 501(c)(3) organizations. From formation through mature operations, NonprofitSuite helps you manage meetings, documents, tasks, and more.

= Free Version Features =

* **User Management** - Custom nonprofit roles (Board Member, Committee Member, Staff, Volunteer, Treasurer, Secretary)
* **Meeting Management** - Schedule board meetings, committee meetings, special meetings, and annual meetings
* **Agenda Builder** - Create structured agendas with drag-and-drop reordering, time allocation, and one-click PDF export
* **Minutes Recorder** - Take comprehensive meeting minutes with attendance tracking, vote recording, approval workflow, and action items
* **Task Management** - Track action items from meetings with assignment, due dates, and status tracking. Convert meeting action items directly to tasks
* **Document Management** - Organize all organizational documents with categories, version control, and access control
* **PDF Export** - Export agendas and minutes to PDF with one click for printing and distribution

= Mobile-First Design =

NonprofitSuite is designed mobile-first, meaning it works great on phones, tablets, and desktops. Take minutes on your phone during meetings, upload documents via camera, and manage tasks on the go.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `nonprofitsuite` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Complete the setup wizard to configure your organization
4. Start managing meetings, documents, and tasks!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is NonprofitSuite really free? =

Yes! The free version includes complete meeting management, agenda building, minutes recording, task management, and document organization. It's free forever, no credit card required.

= What's included in the Pro version? =

Pro ($20/year) adds 31 additional modules including Treasury/Accounting, Donor Management, Volunteer Management, Grant Management, and much more. See our website for the complete feature list.

= Can I use this on my phone? =

Absolutely! NonprofitSuite is designed mobile-first. You can take minutes during meetings on your phone, upload documents via camera, and manage all features from any device.

= What nonprofit types is this designed for? =

NonprofitSuite works for any 501(c)(3) organization including historical societies, community groups, food banks, churches, environmental groups, arts organizations, and more.

= Do I need any other plugins? =

No! NonprofitSuite works out of the box with no dependencies. The free version uses built-in WordPress features. Pro version adds optional integrations.

= Where is my data stored? =

All data is stored in your WordPress database. You control your data. Pro version optionally integrates with Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive for document storage.

= Is there a limit on users or meetings? =

No limits! Add as many users, meetings, documents, and tasks as you need.

= Can I export my data? =

Yes! Export agendas and minutes to PDF. Pro version includes comprehensive data export options.
